Indonesia's Flavor Guide: From Streets to Feasts

Covers the spectrum of Indonesian cuisine, from street food to high-end dining.

Jakarta Region

Restoran Sate Khas Senayan

Address: Jalan M.H. Thamrin No. 30, Jakarta, 10350

Cuisine: Indonesian (Satay)

Must Try: Sate Ayam, Sate Kambing, Gado-Gado

A renowned chain serving traditional Indonesian satay, offering a variety of skewered meats and flavorful peanut sauce.

Dapur Asia

Address: Jalan Tendean Raya No. 3, Jakarta, 12790

Cuisine: Asian (Indonesian Fusion)

Must Try: Nasi Goreng, Ayam Penyet, Sambal Matah

A perfect blend of traditional and contemporary flavors, offering a variety of Indonesian fusion dishes.

Restoran Bubur Ayam

Address: Jalan Cikini Raya No. 11, Jakarta, 10310

Cuisine: Indonesian (Chicken Porridge)

Must Try: Bubur Ayam, Kerupuk, Ayam Goreng

Famous for its comforting chicken porridge, served with crispy crackers and fried chicken.

Bali Region

Mama San Bali

Address: Jalan Petitenget No. 100X, Seminyak, Bali, 80361

Cuisine: Asian (Indonesian Fusion)

Must Try: Nasi Campur, Beef Rendang, Gado-Gado

A popular restaurant known for its contemporary twist on traditional Indonesian dishes in a chic setting.

Warung Babi Guling

Address: Jalan Raya Kedewatan No. 5, Ubud, Bali, 80571

Cuisine: Balinese (Roast Pork)

Must Try: Babi Guling, Sate Lilit, Nasi Campur

A famed spot for the traditional Balinese dish, Babi Guling (roast pork), served with rice and spicy sambal.

Locavore

Address: Jalan Dewi Sita No. 10, Ubud, Bali, 80571

Cuisine: Indonesian (Modern Fusion)

Must Try: Tahu Tempe, Pork Belly, Suckling Pig

Known for using local ingredients to create modern twists on traditional Indonesian dishes, offering a fine dining experience.

Yogyakarta Region

Restoran Rawon Nguling

Address: Jalan Raya Nguling No. 20, Yogyakarta, 55188

Cuisine: Indonesian (East Javanese)

Must Try: Rawon, Nasi Uduk, Sate Ayam

A beloved spot for traditional East Javanese Rawon, a beef soup served with steamed rice and sambal.

Soto Ayam Kadipiro

Address: Jalan Kadipiro No. 25, Yogyakarta, 55261

Cuisine: Indonesian (Soto)

Must Try: Soto Ayam, Nasi Goreng, Tempeh

Known for its flavorful Soto Ayam (chicken soup), served with rice, lime, and a touch of sambal.

Rujak Cingur

Address: Jalan Cingur No. 5, Yogyakarta, 55124

Cuisine: Indonesian (East Javanese)

Must Try: Rujak Cingur, Sate Kambing, Nasi Pecel

Famous for its Rujak Cingur, a salad made with boiled vegetables, tofu, and the signature cingur (beef nose).

Bandung Region

Sate Tulangan

Address: Jalan Raya No. 12, Bandung, 40123

Cuisine: Indonesian (Satay)

Must Try: Sate Kambing, Tahu Tempe, Sate Ayam

Known for its tender satay, especially goat satay, paired with fresh rice and sambal.

Nasi Goreng Bandung

Address: Jalan Bandoeng No. 9, Bandung, 40221

Cuisine: Indonesian (Nasi Goreng)

Must Try: Nasi Goreng, Nasi Kuning, Sate Ayam

A popular choice for Nasi Goreng, fried rice served with a variety of Indonesian side dishes and skewers.

Ayam Penyet

Address: Jalan Penyet No. 15, Bandung, 40323

Cuisine: Indonesian (Fried Chicken)

Must Try: Ayam Penyet, Tahu Tempe, Sambal Terasi

Known for its Ayam Penyet (fried chicken), served smashed with rice and spicy sambal.
