Singapore's Flavor Guide: From Streets to Feasts

Covers the spectrum of Singaporean cuisine, from street food to high-end dining.

Downtown Core

Lau Pa Sat

Address: 18 Raffles Quay, Singapore 048582

Cuisine: Singaporean (Hawker Center)

Must Try: Satay, Hainanese Chicken Rice, Laksa

A vibrant hawker center offering a wide range of local favorites, including the famous Satay and Hainanese Chicken Rice.

Chatterbox

Address: Hilton Singapore Orchard, 581 Orchard Road, Singapore 238883

Cuisine: Singaporean (Chinese)

Must Try: Mandarin Chicken Rice, Salted Egg Yolk Crab, Wok-fried Lobster

Known for its premium Mandarin Chicken Rice, this upscale restaurant combines traditional flavors with elegant service.

Tian Tian Hainanese

Address: 1 Kadayanallur Street, #01-10/11 Maxwell Food Centre, Singapore 069184

Cuisine: Singaporean (Hainanese)

Must Try: Hainanese Chicken Rice

This legendary stall is famous for its fragrant, tender Hainanese Chicken Rice, a beloved local dish.

Chinatown

Singapore River

Address: 30 Boat Quay, Singapore 049818

Cuisine: Singaporean (Local Seafood)

Must Try: Chili Crab, Black Pepper Crab, Salted Egg Crab

Located along the river, this restaurant is renowned for its iconic Chili Crab and other flavorful seafood dishes.

Maxwell Hawker Centre

Address: 1 Kadayanallur Street, Singapore 069184

Cuisine: Singaporean (Local Hawker Food)

Must Try: Chicken Rice, Char Kway Teow, Laksa

A bustling hawker center offering a variety of local dishes, with the famous Tian Tian Hainanese Chicken Rice being a must-try.

Liao Feng

Address: 137 Telok Ayer Street, Singapore 068602

Cuisine: Singaporean (Chinese)

Must Try: Dim Sum, Char Siew, Wanton Noodles

A favorite spot for dim sum and authentic Cantonese dishes, offering a taste of traditional Chinese flavors in the heart of Chinatown.

Cuisine: International (Mediterranean)

Orchard Road

Soup Brothers

Address: 313 Orchard Road, Singapore 238895

Cuisine: International (Soup and Sandwiches)

Must Try: Tomato Basil Soup, Roasted Chicken Sandwich, Clam Chowder

A cozy spot serving comforting soups and sandwiches, perfect for a quick and satisfying meal while shopping along Orchard Road.

Hoshigaoka

Address: 391 Orchard Road, Singapore 238874

Cuisine: Japanese

Must Try: Sashimi, Ramen, Tempura

A sophisticated Japanese restaurant offering a wide range of sushi, sashimi, and other Japanese specialties in the heart of Orchard Road.

Sushi Tei

Address: 302 Orchard Road, Singapore 238862

Cuisine: Japanese

Must Try: Sushi Platter, Sashimi, Chirashi Don

An iconic sushi chain offering fresh and delicious sushi and sashimi in a cozy, modern setting.
