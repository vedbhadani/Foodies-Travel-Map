Tokyo Region

Sukiyabashi Jiro

Address: Basement Floor, Tsukamoto Sogyo Building, 2-15-10 Ginza, Chuo City, Tokyo, 104-0061

Cuisine: Sushi

Must Try: Omakase Sushi, Uni (Sea Urchin), Toro (Fatty Tuna)

A world-renowned sushi restaurant led by Jiro Ono, famous for its precision and perfection in sushi-making.

Narisawa

Address: 2-6-15 Minami Aoyama, Minato City, Tokyo, 107-0062

Cuisine: Innovative Japanese

Must Try: Satoyama Scenery Dish, Foie Gras Monaka, Charcoal-grilled Wagyu

A Michelin-starred dining experience blending sustainability with cutting-edge Japanese culinary techniques.

Ichiran Ramen Shinjuku

Address: 1-22-7 Kabukicho, Shinjuku City, Tokyo, 160-0021

Cuisine: Ramen

Must Try: Tonkotsu Ramen, Customizable Broth, Thin Sliced Pork

Famous for its solo dining booths and customizable ramen, perfect for enjoying a focused culinary experience.

Kyoto Region

Kikunoi Honten

Address: Shimokawaracho, Higashiyama Ward, Kyoto, 605-0825

Cuisine: Kaiseki

Must Try: Hassun Seasonal Platter, Matsutake Dobin Mushi, Grilled Ayu

A traditional kaiseki restaurant offering a culinary journey through Kyoto’s seasonal ingredients.

Gion Sasaki

Address: 570-123 Gionmachi Minamigawa, Higashiyama Ward, Kyoto, 605-0074

Cuisine: Kaiseki

Must Try: Seasonal Omakase Menu, Grilled Eel, Yudofu (Tofu Hot Pot)

Known for its innovative take on traditional kaiseki, blending artistry and flavor.

Nishiki Warai

Address: Nishiki Market, Nakagyo Ward, Kyoto, 604-8051

Cuisine: Japanese Street Food

Must Try: Okonomiyaki, Kyoto-style Yaki Udon, Egg Tofu

A lively eatery in the heart of Kyoto’s iconic Nishiki Market, serving classic street food dishes.

Osaka Region

Mizuno

Address: 1-4-15 Dotonbori, Chuo Ward, Osaka, 542-0071

Cuisine: Okonomiyaki

Must Try: Yamaimo-yaki, Pork and Seafood Okonomiyaki, Negiyaki

A family-run eatery serving Osaka’s best savory pancakes since 1945.

Harukoma Sushi

Address: 5-5-2 Tenjinbashi, Kita Ward, Osaka, 530-0041

Cuisine: Sushi

Must Try: Otoro (Fatty Tuna), Ebi (Shrimp), Anago (Sea Eel)

A bustling sushi restaurant with fresh, generously sized sushi at affordable prices.

Kuromon Ichiba Market

Address: 2 Chome-4-1 Nipponbashi, Chuo Ward, Osaka, 542-0073

Cuisine: Street Food

Must Try: Grilled Scallops, Takoyaki, Fresh Sashimi

Known as "Osaka's Kitchen," offering a variety of fresh seafood and local delicacies.

Hokkaido Region

Sapporo Beer Garden

Address: 9-2-10 Kita 7 Johigashi, Higashi Ward, Sapporo, Hokkaido, 065-0007

Cuisine: Japanese BBQ (Jingisukan)

Must Try: Jingisukan (Grilled Mutton), Hokkaido Crab, Sapporo Beer Pairings

A historic brewery-turned-restaurant offering Hokkaido’s famous mutton BBQ and fresh beer.

Hanamaru Sushi

Address: 6-3-2 Kita 6 Jonishi, Chuo Ward, Sapporo, Hokkaido, 060-0806

Cuisine: Conveyor Belt Sushi

Must Try: Salmon Belly, Hotate (Scallop), Ikura (Salmon Roe)

Known for its high-quality sushi served fresh on a conveyor belt, perfect for casual dining.

Ramen Shingen

Address: 7 Chome Minami 6 Jonishi, Chuo Ward, Sapporo, Hokkaido, 064-0806

Cuisine: Ramen

Must Try: Miso Ramen, Butter Corn Ramen, Chashu Pork

Famous for its rich and creamy miso ramen, a signature of Sapporo's culinary scene.

Okinawa Region

Makishi Public Market Restaurants

Address: 2-10-1 Matsuo, Naha City, Okinawa, 900-0014

Cuisine: Okinawan

Must Try: Goya Champuru, Rafute (Braised Pork Belly), Umibudo (Sea Grapes)

Experience authentic Okinawan flavors in the vibrant market atmosphere.

Shima Donuts Café

Address: 187-6 Maesato, Ishigaki, Okinawa, 907-0002

Cuisine: Café/Desserts

Must Try: Black Sugar Donuts, Purple Sweet Potato Ice Cream, Okinawan Tea

A cozy café specializing in Okinawan-inspired donuts and desserts made from local ingredients.

Zanpa Lounge

Address: 1575 Uza, Yomitan, Nakagami District, Okinawa, 904-0328

Cuisine: Local Okinawan

Must Try: Mozuku Tempura, Okinawan Soba, Shikwasa Drinks

A serene lounge offering a mix of traditional Okinawan dishes with stunning coastal views.
