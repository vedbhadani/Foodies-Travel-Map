India's Flavor Guide: From Streets to Feasts

Covers the spectrum of Indian cuisine, from street food to high-end dining.

North India

Bukhara

Address: ITC Maurya, Sardar Patel Marg, Diplomatic Enclave, Chanakyapuri, New Delhi, 110021

Cuisine: North-West Frontier

Must Try: Dal Bukhara, Sikandari Raan, Tandoori Naan

World-renowned for its slow-cooked lentils and rustic kebabs. An unparalleled dining experience in traditional Indian flavors.

Karim's

Address: 16, Gali Kababian, Jama Masjid, Old Delhi, New Delhi, 110006

Cuisine: Mughlai

Must Try: Mutton Burra, Mughlai Biryani, Chicken Jahangiri

A historic gem offering the authentic taste of Mughal cuisine in the bustling streets of Old Delhi.

Indian Accent

Address: The Lodhi, Lodhi Road, New Delhi, 110003

Cuisine: Modern Indian

Must Try: Meetha Achaar Pork Ribs, Kashmiri Morels, Blue Cheese Naan

Combines innovation with tradition, serving creative twists on Indian classics with global inspirations.

West India

Trishna

Address: 7, Sai Baba Marg, Kala Ghoda, Fort, Mumbai, Maharashtra, 400001

Cuisine: Coastal (Seafood)

Must Try: Butter Garlic Crab, Bombay Duck Fry, Prawns Koliwada

A seafood paradise celebrated for its flavorful and fresh coastal delicacies in Mumbai's cultural hub.

The Bombay Canteen

Address: Unit 1, Process House, Kamala Mills, S.B. Road, Lower Parel, Mumbai, Maharashtra, 400013

Cuisine: Contemporary Indian

Must Try: Arbi Tuk, Chettinad-style Chicken, Gulab Nut

A trendy eatery blending nostalgic Indian flavors with creative presentations in a lively atmosphere.

Shree Thaker Bhojanalay

Address: 31, Dadiseth Agyari Lane, Kalbadevi, Mumbai, Maharashtra, 400002

Cuisine: Gujarati Thali

Must Try: Gujarati Thali, Farsan, Aamras (seasonal)

Known for its unlimited thalis featuring traditional Gujarati dishes served with warmth and hospitality.

South India

Dakshin, ITC Grand Chola

Address: ITC Grand Chola, 63, Mount Road, Guindy, Chennai, Tamil Nadu, 600032

Cuisine: South Indian (Regional Specialties)

Must Try: Meen Moilee, Kozhi Varuval, Appam with Stew

A fine-dining experience showcasing the culinary diversity of South India with authentic flavors and refined service.

MTR (Mavalli Tiffin Rooms)

Address: 14, Lalbagh Main Road, Mavalli, Bangalore, Karnataka, 560004

Cuisine: South Indian

Must Try: Masala Dosa, Rava Idli, Filter Coffee

A historic eatery offering classic South Indian breakfasts and snacks with an unchanging recipe since 1924.

Paragon

Address: Kannur Road, Near Mavoor Road Junction, Kozhikode, Kerala, 673001

Cuisine: Malabar Cuisine

Must Try: Malabar Chicken Biryani, Prawn Roast, Kerala Parotta

Kerala's most loved restaurant for its legendary biryanis and bold coastal flavors.

East India

Oh! Calcutta

Address: Forum Mall, 10/3, Elgin Road, Kolkata, West Bengal, 700020

Cuisine: Bengali

Must Try: Kosha Mangsho, Hilsa Fish Curry, Chingri Malai Curry

A premium spot for savoring authentic Bengali cuisine with seasonal highlights like hilsa dishes.

Kewpie's Kitchen

Address: 2, Elgin Lane, Near Netaji Bhavan Metro Station, Kolkata, West Bengal, 700020

Cuisine: Homestyle Bengali

Must Try: Shorshe Ilish, Luchi-Alur Dum, Chhanar Dalna

Intimate and charming, serving homemade-style Bengali meals in a traditional setting.
