South Korea's Flavor Guide: From Streets to Feasts

Covers the spectrum of Korean cuisine, from street food to high-end dining.

Seoul

Myeongdong Kyoja

Address: 29-1 Myeongdong 2(i)-ga, Jung-gu, Seoul, South Korea

Cuisine: Korean (Noodles)

Must Try: Kalguksu (Knife-Cut Noodles), Mandu (Dumplings), Bibimbap

A beloved institution serving flavorful handmade noodles and dumplings in the heart of Myeongdong.

P.S.B (Pork Soup Bar)

Address: 20-1, Itaewon-ro 55-gil, Yongsan-gu, Seoul, South Korea

Cuisine: Korean (Pork Soup)

Must Try: Gamjatang (Pork Bone Soup), Kimchi, Steamed Rice

Famous for its hearty pork bone soup, a flavorful dish perfect for cold weather and loved by locals.

Banchan House

Address: 2-5 Gwanghwamun, Jongno-gu, Seoul, South Korea

Cuisine: Korean (Banchan and BBQ)

Must Try: Banchan (Side Dishes), Samgyeopsal (Grilled Pork Belly), Kimchi Jeon (Kimchi Pancakes)

A traditional Korean BBQ restaurant known for its delicious banchan and freshly grilled meats.

Busan

Jagalchi Fish Market

Address: 52 Jagalchi-ro, Jung-gu, Busan, South Korea

Cuisine: Korean (Seafood)

Must Try: Live Octopus, Grilled Fish, Raw Fish Platters

Located in the famous Jagalchi Market, this spot offers fresh seafood with the option to enjoy live octopus and grilled fish.

Busan Beach Restaurant

Address: 210 Haeundaehaebyeon-ro, Haeundae-gu, Busan, South Korea

Cuisine: Korean (Seafood and BBQ)

Must Try: Haeundae Fish Stew, Grilled Shellfish, Sashimi

A beachfront eatery offering fresh seafood and BBQ dishes with a view of the beautiful Haeundae Beach.

Southern Korea BBQ

Address: 43-4, Geumryeong-ro, Busan, South Korea

Cuisine: Korean (BBQ)

Must Try: Samgyeopsal, Bulgogi, Kimchi Stew

A local favorite for traditional Korean BBQ, offering a variety of meats grilled at your table alongside flavorful stews and sides.

Jeju Island

Jeju Black Pork Street

Address: 43 Seogwipo-si, Jeju, South Korea

Cuisine: Korean (Jeju Black Pork)

Must Try: Jeju Black Pork BBQ, Grilled Vegetables, Kimchi

A unique dining experience featuring the famous Jeju Black Pork, served with an assortment of side dishes and grilled to perfection.

Ocheon

Address: 35-9 Ocheon-ro, Seogwipo-si, Jeju, South Korea

Cuisine: Korean (Seafood)

Must Try: Raw Fish, Abalone Soup, Grilled Fish

A popular spot for fresh seafood, known for its raw fish dishes and delicious abalone soup.

Jeju Cafe

Incheon

Incheon Grill

Address: 12-11 Incheon-ro, Incheon, South Korea

Cuisine: Korean (BBQ)

Must Try: Bulgogi, Grilled Short Ribs, Kimchi Pancakes

A local gem for Korean BBQ with excellent grilled meats and traditional banchan.

Incheon Bibimbap

Address: 32-4, Namdong-gu, Incheon, South Korea

Cuisine: Korean (Bibimbap)

Must Try: Bibimbap, Kimchi, Fried Egg

Famous for its bibimbap served with fresh vegetables, rice, and a fried egg, a comforting and flavorful meal.

Incheon Seafood

Address: 78-12, Haeundae, Incheon, South Korea

Cuisine: Korean (Seafood)

Must Try: Grilled Clams, Seafood Pancakes, Fish Stew

This seafood restaurant offers an array of fresh seafood dishes, including grilled clams and seafood pancakes, perfect for seafood lovers.
