China's Flavor Guide: From Streets to Feasts

Covers the spectrum of Chinese cuisine, from street food to high-end dining.

Beijing Region

Donglaishun

Address: 3 Qianmen Dong Street, Beijing, 100000

Cuisine: Hot Pot

Must Try: Beijing-style Hot Pot, Lamb Skewers, Handmade Noodles

Famous for its traditional Beijing hot pot with a unique combination of spices and fresh ingredients.

Quanjude

Address: 32 Qianmen Street, Beijing, 100051

Cuisine: Peking Duck

Must Try: Peking Duck, Scallion Pancakes, Sweet Bean Paste

World-renowned for its Peking Duck, offering crispy skin and tender meat with traditional accompaniments.

Duck de Chine

Address: 194 Dongcheng District, Beijing, 100010

Cuisine: Peking Duck

Must Try: Peking Duck, Dim Sum, Braised Pork Belly

A modern take on the classic Peking Duck, offering both a traditional and contemporary dining experience.

Shanghai Region

Din Tai Fung

Address: 123 Xintiandi, Shanghai, 200020

Cuisine: Taiwanese

Must Try: Xiaolongbao (Soup Dumplings), Fried Rice, Shrimp Dumplings

Famous for its delicate soup dumplings and other finely crafted dim sum dishes.

Jiangnan

Address: 128 The Bund, Shanghai, 200002

Cuisine: Suzhou Cuisine

Must Try: Sweet and Sour Mandarin Fish, Steamed Pork, Shrimp Dumplings

Serving traditional Suzhou cuisine with fresh, flavorful dishes and a refined atmosphere.

Ultraviolet by Paul Pairet

Address: 18, 6th Floor, Central Tower, Shanghai, 200123

Cuisine: Modern Chinese

Must Try: Multi-sensory dining experience, Fusion Dishes

An avant-garde restaurant offering a one-of-a-kind dining experience with immersive visuals and sound.

Guangzhou Region

Tangjia Xiang

Address: 32 Shahe Road, Guangzhou, 510000

Cuisine: Cantonese

Must Try: Dim Sum, Roast Duck, Wonton Noodles

A popular spot for classic Cantonese dim sum and fresh seafood dishes.

Lei Yue Min

Address: 48 Shangxiajiu Street, Guangzhou, 510000

Cuisine: Cantonese

Must Try: Cantonese Roast Goose, Hot Pot, Spring Rolls

Known for its traditional Cantonese roast meats and rich, flavorful broths.

Chun Cai

Address: 102 Beijing Road, Guangzhou, 510000

Cuisine: Cantonese

Must Try: Cantonese Dim Sum, Fish Maw Soup, Claypot Rice

A refined restaurant offering the best of Cantonese cuisine with a modern twist.

Sichuan Region

Huoguo

Address: 9 Sichuan Road, Chengdu, Sichuan, 610000

Cuisine: Sichuan Hot Pot

Must Try: Spicy Hot Pot, Mapo Tofu, Kung Pao Chicken

A hotspot for authentic Sichuan hot pot, known for its bold, spicy flavors.

Dujiangyan

Address: 58 Hongxing Road, Chengdu, Sichuan, 610000

Cuisine: Sichuan

Must Try: Mapo Tofu, Spicy Dumplings, Sichuan-style Lamb

Renowned for its authentic Sichuan hot and numbing dishes that are full of flavor.

Long Chuan

Address: 35 Shuanglin Road, Chengdu, Sichuan, 610000

Cuisine: Sichuan

Must Try: Hot and Sour Soup, Dandan Noodles, Wontons

Specializes in traditional Sichuan cuisine with a focus on bold flavors and spicy ingredients.
