Vietnam's Flavor Guide: From Streets to Feasts

Covers the spectrum of Vietnamese cuisine, from street food to high-end dining.

Hanoi Region

Quan An Ngon

Address: 18 Phan Boi Chau, Hoan Kiem, Hanoi, 100000

Cuisine: Vietnamese Street Food

Must Try: Pho Bo (Beef Noodle Soup), Goi Cuon (Vietnamese Spring Rolls), Banh Xeo (Vietnamese Pancakes)

A popular eatery offering a wide variety of traditional Vietnamese street food in a charming setting.

Cha Ca La Vong

Address: 14 Cha Ca Street, Hanoi, 100000

Cuisine: Hanoi Specialty

Must Try: Cha Ca (Grilled Fish with Dill), Banh Cuon (Rice Rolls), Vietnamese Iced Coffee

Famous for its unique Cha Ca, a traditional Hanoi fish dish, served with fresh herbs and vermicelli.

Pho 2000

Address: 1-3 Phan Chu Trinh, Hanoi, 100000

Cuisine: Vietnamese

Must Try: Pho (Vietnamese Noodle Soup), Goi Cuon (Spring Rolls), Banh Mi

A popular destination for pho lovers, serving delicious bowls of traditional Vietnamese noodle soup.

Ho Chi Minh City Region

Ben Thanh Market

Address: Le Loi Street, District 1, Ho Chi Minh City, 700000

Cuisine: Vietnamese Street Food

Must Try: Banh Xeo (Vietnamese Pancakes), Pho, Goi Cuon (Spring Rolls)

A vibrant market offering a variety of fresh Vietnamese dishes, perfect for street food lovers.

Quan Com 22

Address: 22 Nguyen Thi Minh Khai, District 1, Ho Chi Minh City, 700000

Cuisine: Vietnamese

Must Try: Com tam (Broken Rice), Goi Vit (Duck Salad), Hu Tieu

A local favorite for traditional Vietnamese dishes, especially their famous broken rice with grilled pork.

3 Dao

Address: 43 Hoang Sa, District 1, Ho Chi Minh City, 700000

Cuisine: Vietnamese Street Food

Must Try: Banh Mi, Goi Cuon (Spring Rolls), Grilled Pork

A must-visit for an authentic Vietnamese street food experience with fresh and flavorful dishes.

Hue Region

Ngu Binh

Address: 35 Nguyen Sinh Cung, Hue, 530000

Cuisine: Royal Vietnamese Cuisine

Must Try: Bun Bo Hue (Spicy Beef Noodle Soup), Nem Lui (Grilled Pork Skewers), Com Hen (Rice with Mussels)

Offering authentic Hue royal cuisine, featuring intricate flavors and a touch of history in every dish.

Thanh Tam

Address: 27 Ly Thuong Kiet, Hue, 530000

Cuisine: Vietnamese

Must Try: Banh Beo (Rice Cake), Goi Cuon (Spring Rolls), Banh Khoai (Hue Pancakes)

Famous for its traditional dishes from the Imperial city of Hue, with a focus on delicate, fresh flavors.

Da Nang Region

Address: 61 Le Duan, Da Nang, 550000

Cuisine: Vietnamese

Must Try: Mi Quang (Quang Noodles), Banh Xeo (Vietnamese Pancakes), Grilled Fish

Cuisine: Vietnamese
