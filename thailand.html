THAILAND's Flavor Guide: From Streets to Feasts

Covers the spectrum of Indian cuisine, from street food to high-end dining.

Bangkok Region

Dusit Thani

Address: 946 Rama IV Road, Dusit, Bangkok, 10300

Cuisine: Thai Fine Dining

Must Try: Tom Yum Goong, Green Curry, Pad Thai

A luxurious dining experience offering the best of traditional Thai flavors with a contemporary twist.

Somtum Der

Address: 5, Soi Sukhumvit 12, Sukhumvit Road, Bangkok, 10110

Cuisine: Issan

Must Try: Som Tum (Papaya Salad), Sticky Rice, Grilled Pork Neck

A modern take on Northeastern Thai (Issan) cuisine, offering bold, spicy dishes that showcase local ingredients.

Bo.Lan

Address: 24 Sukhumvit Soi 53, Bangkok, 10110

Cuisine: Modern Thai

Must Try: Mhor Thalay (Fish Soup), Grilled Beef Salad, Papaya Salad with Blue Crab

A Michelin-starred restaurant blending traditional Thai recipes with modern cooking techniques, focusing on sustainability.

Chiang Mai Region

Khao Soi Mae Suan

Address: 4/1 Prapokklao Road, Chiang Mai, 50200

Cuisine: Northern Thai

Must Try: Khao Soi (Curry Noodle Soup), Sai Ua (Northern Thai Sausage), Larp

Specializing in the iconic Khao Soi, this restaurant brings the flavors of Northern Thailand to your plate.

Spices and Herbs

Address: 2/5 Nimmanhaemin Road, Chiang Mai, 50200

Cuisine: Thai Fusion

Must Try: Spicy Basil Chicken, Mango Sticky Rice, Green Curry

Offering a unique fusion of flavors from across Thailand, with an emphasis on fresh herbs and spices.

The Cooking School Chiang Mai

Address: 72/3 Chiang Mai-Lampang Road, Chiang Mai, 50200

Cuisine: Thai Culinary Experience

Must Try: Massaman Curry, Pad Kee Mao, Thai Sweets

A hands-on culinary experience where guests can learn how to cook traditional Thai dishes with expert instructors.

Phuket Region

Patong Beach Restaurant

Address: Patong Beach, Phuket, 83150

Cuisine: Seafood

Must Try: Grilled Lobster, Fish Curry, Shrimp Cakes

A beachfront seafood restaurant serving fresh catches of the day, with stunning views of the Andaman Sea.

Blue Elephant Phuket

Address: 96 Krabi Road, Phuket Town, 83000

Cuisine: Royal Thai

Must Try: Tom Kha Gai (Coconut Soup), Red Curry, Pad Thai

A renowned restaurant specializing in authentic Thai royal cuisine, offering elegant dining in a traditional setting.

Pineapple Restaurant

Address: 40/1 Patak Road, Karon Beach, Phuket, 83100

Cuisine: Thai Fusion

Must Try: Pineapple Fried Rice, Spicy Seafood Soup, Mango Salad

A vibrant fusion restaurant known for its creative use of tropical ingredients and flavorful Thai dishes.

Koh Samui Region

Jiwa Restaurant

Address: 123/1 Moo 3, Lamai Beach, Koh Samui, 84310

Cuisine: Thai Fine Dining

Must Try: Grilled Tiger Prawns, Papaya Salad, Massaman Curry

An upscale dining experience offering creative and beautifully plated Thai dishes, with a modern twist.

The White Olive

Address: 32/3 Chaweng Beach, Koh Samui, 84320

Cuisine: Mediterranean Thai Fusion

Must Try: Greek Salad, Red Curry Pizza, Olive Tapenade

A Mediterranean-inspired Thai fusion restaurant offering a unique twist on classic dishes from both cuisines.

Koh Samui Café

Address: 44/1 Bophut, Koh Samui, 84320

Cuisine: Café/Desserts

Must Try: Coconut Cake, Thai Iced Tea, Mango Sticky Rice

A cozy café with a laid-back atmosphere, serving a variety of Thai-inspired desserts and refreshing beverages.
